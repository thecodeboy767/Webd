Otaku Critic Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy like we respect the art of anime!
Trust us, your data is safe!

1. What Information We Collect

Just like we collect anime recommendations, here’s what we collect to keep your experience smooth:

  • Your Profile Info: When you sign up, we gather your username, email, and other fun info to help us connect you with fellow anime lovers.
  • Your Reviews & Comments: We store your anime reviews, ratings, and comments so you can keep sharing your thoughts with the community.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Yes, we use cookies, but not the edible kind! They help us personalize your experience and keep track of your anime preferences.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data to make your anime experience even better, just like how a good anime plot gets better with each episode:

  • Personalized Experience: We use your info to suggest anime you'll love. We promise it’s all top-tier, no filler episodes.
  • Community Building: Share reviews, talk about the latest episodes, and connect with other Otakus who are just as obsessed as you!
  • Improvement: Your data helps us make the site faster, cooler, and more anime-friendly. We use it to fix bugs and add new features.
  • Communication: We might send you awesome updates or news about the anime world. But don’t worry, you can unsubscribe anytime!

3. Protecting Your Data

Your privacy is our number one priority! We use secure measures to protect your data from any villains (aka hackers). Though nothing is totally invincible, we’ve got our defenses up, just like your favorite anime protagonist!

4. Sharing Your Data

We don’t share your info with any shady characters. But in these rare scenarios, we might:

  • With Partners: We work with trustworthy partners who help us run the site, like web hosting or support services.
  • If Legally Required: If the law comes knocking, we might have to share your info (but we’ll only do it if absolutely necessary).

5. Your Rights (You're the Hero!)

Just like any protagonist, you’ve got control over your data:

  • Access: Check out your personal info anytime from your profile!
  • Update: Change your preferences, favorite anime, and profile info whenever you like.
  • Delete: Want to go off the grid? You can delete your account anytime. We’ll miss you, but we respect your choice!
  • Opt-Out: Get no more emails? We get it! You can unsubscribe at any time.

6. Cookies (Not the Yummy Kind)

We use cookies (the techy kind) to help us remember your preferences and keep things running smooth. You can manage them in your browser, but we recommend keeping them so you can continue your seamless anime journey!

7. Third-Party Links

Sometimes we link to other cool sites, but please remember: we’re not responsible for their privacy policies. Always check them out before sharing your personal info!

8. Kids' Privacy (For the Young Otaku Fans)

Our site is for Otakus aged 13 or older. If you're younger than that, please don't share any personal info with us. If we find out we’ve collected information from someone under 13, we’ll delete it faster than you can say "Kawaii!"

9. Updates to This Policy

We might update this policy from time to time. But don’t worry, we’ll always keep you updated on any big changes. No plot twists here—just clear updates!

10. Questions? Let’s Talk!

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, reach out to us. We’re here for all our fellow Otaku!
